## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate and secure the Goldentree services.

Account information:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password or temporary password created for account access
- Account status, such as active or inactive

Login and authentication information:

- Email address and password submitted during login
- Authentication token issued after successful login
- Basic account identifiers needed to keep the session secure

Customer enquiry information, where you submit it through our website or customer contact channels:

- Name
- Phone number
- Email address, if provided
- Message or enquiry details

App usage and technical information:

- Device and browser information needed to deliver app content
- Server logs such as request time, IP address, endpoint requested, and error information
- App interaction data needed to diagnose issues and maintain security

Catalogue and content data:

- Products, rates, offers, gallery items, showroom information, and other content loaded from Goldentree systems.

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect Through The Mobile App

The current Goldentree mobile app does not request access to:

- Precise device location
- Camera
- Photos or media library
- Contacts
- Microphone
- Calendar
- Payment card information
- Biometric data
- Health data

The app may open external map, phone, WhatsApp, or browser links when you choose to contact or navigate to a showroom. Those external services may process information under their own privacy policies.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Create and manage private customer accounts
- Authenticate customer login
- Display live gold rates, jewellery catalogues, offers, and showroom information
- Respond to customer enquiries and support requests
- Maintain, secure, debug, and improve the app, website, admin panel, and backend systems
- Prevent unauthorized access, misuse, fraud, and security incidents
- Comply with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations

## 5. Legal Basis For Processing

Where applicable, we process personal information based on:

- Consent, where we ask for it

## 6. Sharing Of Information

We do not sell personal information.

## 7. Data Storage And Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. Passwords are stored in hashed form. Authentication tokens are used to protect private account access. Access to admin tools and customer data is limited to authorized users.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including account management, customer support, legal compliance, security, dispute resolution, and business records.

When information is no longer required, we will delete it, anonymize it, or restrict access according to applicable law and our operational requirements.
